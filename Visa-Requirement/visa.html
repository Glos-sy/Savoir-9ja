VISA REQUIREMENTS OVERVIEW

Essential guidelines for securing a visa to Nigeria

What documents are required to apply for a Nigerian visa ?

To apply for a Nigerian visa, you’ll need a valid passport (with at least six months validity), a completed application form, recent passport photos, and proof of accommodation.

Are there different types of visas for Nigeria?

Nigeria offers different visa types based on travel purpose—tourist, business, student, and transit visas. Each has specific requirements, so choosing the right one ensures a smooth application process.

How long does it take to obtain a Nigerian visa?

Visa Application Steps

A clear guide to navigate the visa application process

Gather Required Documents

Gather all required documents as listed in the visa requirements, ensuring your passport is valid and your application form is accurately completed. Include supporting documents like photographs, proof of accommodation, and any other necessary paperwork.

Submit Your Application

Submit your application online or at the nearest Nigerian embassy/consulate. If applying online, follow the official portal instructions and pay the required fees. For in-person submissions, schedule an appointment if needed and organize your documents properly.
